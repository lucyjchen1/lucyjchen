PLAYGROUND

Foods of St. Louis is a package of 12 postcards the show the diversity of food culture in St. Louis. Many of the foods that are significant to St. Louis come from accidental creations, so this card and packaging design reflect that playfulness. 

The visual language of the cards use photography, illustration, and expressive typography to capture each food. On the back of each card is a description of the food portrayed and where in St. Louis it can be found.
