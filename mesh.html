Mesh is an annual magazine, produced by the Contemporary Art Museum of St. Louis, featuring the exhibitions of the next year and highlighting things. I designed the layout and typeset the magazine for CAM's 2022-2023 issue. In addition to the main issue I designed a wordmark and spread for the 10th celebration of the Great Rivers Biennial. This wordmark was also used in a t-shirt design and branding for an event.
