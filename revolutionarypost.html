Just for kicks
The Revolutionary Post is a written representation of a podcast episode from 99% Invisible. This work showcases the interviewer with short sheets of primary sources related to the original episode. The story also touts the American post system as responsible for the American Revolution. As such the book is designed to fit in a standard postal envelope and help promote using the postal system especially during a time when the postal system was __going under.
