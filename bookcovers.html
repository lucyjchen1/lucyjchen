A Face Like Glass by Frances Hardinge

This cover emphasizes the setting of the book, in the caves of Caverna. Fraces Hardinge describes Caverna as an entity with thought and ambitions to keep the main character contained. The lettering and texture of the cover reflects the title "A Face like Glass" with a glass-like sheen.

Neverwhere by Neil Gaiman

I wanted to sell the British aspect of this book. In a way, Neil Gaiman gives a fantastical homage to the city of London throughout this book. I chose the element of the London Underground through which the character often use to travel between places as the main imagery while giving it a slightly darker and more sinister feel.
