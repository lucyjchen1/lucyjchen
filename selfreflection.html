Self-Reflection Zine

My classmates and I graduated during the pandemic. Many of my friends were still looking for jobs and feeling anxious. I organized a collaborative zine project where each piece had a different writer, artist, and designer. This project was meant to be fun and care-free and connected many people to work together who had never done so berfore.

Here are some of the pieces I contributed, as well as the cover and table of contents which I designed.
