Project Info
2021
Publication design

Advisor
Amy Auman		 A historical retelling of the interwar period in China and Hong Kong told through the lens of a young woman, Clara. The book hopes to offer digestible historical information especially for readers who are unfamiliar with the events of this period. Short exterior stories are included that relate to and help support the main narrative. I edited the text, which is a transcript of my great, grandmother, research, collected, and designed content of the book.

Advisor
